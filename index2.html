All about Us!


A massive online community of independent filmmakers, music composers, actors, editors etc. use Crafter as a team collaboration app while creating music and movies.

Crafter connects all stakeholders and turns ideas into action through hiring, project scheduling, group conversation, and personalized one on one conversation. Collaborations inspire productivity and in turn stakeholders are more efficient while creating amazing movie and music content.

We do not want artists to only create content we want them to create great entertaining content.

We at Crafter aim to change the way movies and music are created. We want to make this world more entertaining.
